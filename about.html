Microsystems Lab

Studying various physical phenomena through MEMS resonator configuration and exploring the possibility of use of MEMS/NEMS for various engineering applications.

Lab Supervisor

Asst. Prof. Saurabh Chandorkar

Research Interests

  • Energy loss mechanisms in micro/nano scaled resonators
  • Wafer scale packaging for MEMS
  • Human-computer modality enhancements
  • Low cost system development for IC fabrication and characterization
